The Perfect PB & J

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board
    • Optional:
  • Frying Pan

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board
  2. With the butter knife spread peanut butter on slice of bread
  3. With the butter knife spread jelly on the other slice
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half
    6. Optional:
  6. Cook for 4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown, and heated through.
